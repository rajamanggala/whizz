It started
from a single idea.

That what we wear can carry more than just appearance.

We turn ideas into pieces that hold value and meaning. From t-shirts to various merchandise, every piece is created with intention— not always seen, but always present.

We believe anyone can make clothing. But not everyone puts something within it.

Coming from one shared origin, these values continue to move with us— evolving with the present, and shaped through collaborations with artists who bring new perspectives and energy.

WHIZZ is not just about what you wear, but what you carry.
